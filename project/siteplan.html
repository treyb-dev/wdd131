Site Plan: BrewScout

Site Name

Name: BrewScout

The name "BrewScout" blends the idea of discovering local brews (coffee) with the act of scouting out the best spots. It's short, brandable, and clearly aligned with the site’s mission of guiding users to great coffee experiences.

Site Purpose

BrewScout is a local coffee shop directory designed to help users find cafés in their area based on preferences like vegan options, Wi-Fi access, ambiance, or drink specials. The site will feature interactive maps, filterable listings, customer reviews, and blog articles on coffee culture and shop events.

Scenarios

  • Which coffee shops near me have cozy indoor seating and fast Wi-Fi?
  • How can I leave a review or check ratings for my favorite coffee shop?
  • Are there any café events happening this weekend?

Color Scheme

Primary Color: #6f4e37 (used for headers, nav, and logo)

Accent Color: #d9b382 (used for highlights, buttons, and blog headers)

Typography

Main Font: 'Open Sans' - clean, used for body text for readability

Accent Font: 'Playfair Display' - elegant serif for titles and blog headlines
